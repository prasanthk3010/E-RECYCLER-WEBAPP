What we do ?

We act as a interface for collecting the waste cartons from end customers after unwrapping items with also certain criteria like end of return period to be done and encouraging them to give away the packaging waste by introducing schemes like reward points or small discounts. With this, people will come forward and give away the waste cartons and bubble wraps they have which can be recycled by the sellers again instead of making the cartons and other packaging stuffs from scratch.
